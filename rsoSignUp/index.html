RSO Instructor Sign Up
.
1. Open your browser to NRAInstructors.org then select 'Login & Register'.

2. Select 'Register'.

3. Enter your NRA member ID, Last Name, Country and Zip Code.

4. Create your NRA Instructor Login ID and password.

5. Click on the 'Click here to Activate' button and pay for credential.

Once this process is completed you will be able to print your certificate and access other instructor information. Congratulations.
